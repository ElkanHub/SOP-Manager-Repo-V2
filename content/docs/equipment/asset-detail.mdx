Click any equipment row to view its complete details.

---

## Left Column

Shows metadata:
- Asset ID and Name
- Department badges
- Status
- Serial Number and Model
- Linked SOP (if any)
- QR Code for quick scanning

---

## Right Column

- **Next Service card** — Shows upcoming PM due date with urgency colour
- **Log PM Completion button** — Record that maintenance was done
- **Service History** — Table of all PM tasks, completed and pending

---

## Secondary Departments

If you belong to a secondary department for this asset, you can still view and log PM — you are an authorized user even though it is not your primary department.
