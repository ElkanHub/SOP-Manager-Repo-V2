The **Equipment Registry** is where all maintained assets are tracked. Think of it as your organisation's asset database.

---

## Default View

When you open Equipment, you see assets from your department (plus any secondary departments you belong to).

The table shows:
- **Asset ID** — Unique identifier (e.g., EQ-001)
- **Name** — Asset name
- **Department** — Owning department
- **Status** — Current state
- **Frequency** — PM schedule
- **Last Serviced** — Most recent PM
- **Next Due** — Upcoming PM date

---

## Statuses

<StatusBadge status="active" /> **Active** — In service, PM schedule active

<StatusBadge status="pending_qa" /> **Pending QA** — Added, waiting for QA approval

<StatusBadge status="inactive" /> **Inactive** — Decommissioned or out of service

---

## Next Due Colours

The Next Due column uses urgency colours:

- **Normal** — More than 7 days away
- **Amber** — 7 days or less
- **Red** — Overdue

---

## QA Approval Requirement

Like SOPs, equipment requires QA approval before going active. Managers submit equipment for review, and QA Managers approve it.
