When preventive maintenance is performed, it must be logged in the system. This creates the audit trail and schedules the next PM.

---

## Who Can Log

**Anyone** in the primary or secondary department can log PM completion. You do not need to be the assigned person.

---

## How to Log

1. Open the equipment detail page
2. Click **Log PM Completion**
3. Fill in:
   - **Notes** — Required. Describe what was done.
   - **Photo** — Optional. Upload a photo of completed work.
4. Click **Complete**

---

## What Happens After

1. The PM task is marked complete
2. Equipment **Last Serviced** updates to today
3. **Next Due** recalculates based on frequency
4. A **new PM task** is automatically created for the next cycle
5. Service history table updates

<Callout type="info">
  You do not need to be the assigned person to log a completion. If someone else completes the work, they can log it — the completed_by field records who actually did the work.
</Callout>
