Managers can reassign PM tasks when the original assignee is unavailable.

---

## When to Reassign

- Someone is on leave
- Responsibilities have changed
- Work was delegated to someone else

---

## How to Reassign

1. Open the equipment detail page
2. Go to **Service History**
3. Find the task
4. Click the **kebab menu** (three dots)
5. Select **Reassign**
6. Choose from active department members
7. Confirm

---

## After Reassigning

- The task now shows the new assignee's name
- The new assignee will see the task in their upcoming maintenance list
- It is good practice to let the new assignee know directly so they are prepared

The reassignment is recorded in the system for tracking purposes.
