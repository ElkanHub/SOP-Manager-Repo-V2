Managers can reassign PM tasks when the original assignee is unavailable.

---

## When to Reassign

- Someone is on leave
- Responsibilities have changed
- Work was delegated to someone else

---

## How to Reassign

1. Open the equipment detail page
2. Go to **Service History**
3. Find the task
4. Click the **kebab menu** (three dots)
5. Select **Reassign**
6. Choose from active department members
7. Confirm

---

## After Reassigning

- The task shows the new assignee
- The old assignee is notified (in their Pulse)
- No automatic notification — the manager should inform them directly

An audit trail entry is created for the reassignment.
