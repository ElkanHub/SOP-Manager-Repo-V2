Managers can add equipment to the registry. Equipment requires QA approval before becoming active.

---

## Who Can Add

**Managers** can add equipment. Employees can view and log PM but cannot add new equipment.

---

## The Add Equipment Form

The form has three steps:

### Step 1: Basic Information

- **Asset ID** — Unique identifier (format: EQ-XXX)
- **Name** — Descriptive name
- **Serial Number** — Manufacturer serial (optional)
- **Model** — Equipment model (optional)
- **Photo** — Upload an image (optional)

### Step 2: Maintenance

- **Frequency** — How often PM is required
  - Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, or Custom
- **Custom Interval** — If Custom, enter days between PM
- **Last Serviced** — Date of most recent service (important!)

<Callout type="tip">
  The Last Serviced date seeds the entire maintenance schedule. If equipment was recently serviced, enter the actual date. If never serviced, enter today as the start date.
</Callout>

### Step 3: Assignment

- **Initial Assignee** — Who is responsible for PM tasks (required)

---

## After Submission

After you submit:
- Equipment status becomes **Pending QA**
- A QA Manager reviews and approves or requests changes
- When approved, the first PM task is automatically created
