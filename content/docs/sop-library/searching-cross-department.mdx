The SOP Library search feature searches across **all Active SOPs** in your organisation, not just your department. This lets you find procedures from other teams when you need them.

---

## Using Cross-Department Search

1. Go to **SOP Library**
2. Type in the **search bar** at the top

Results appear instantly as you type. Every result shows a **department badge** so you always know which team owns the SOP.

---

## What You Can Do With Cross-Department SOPs

When you open a cross-department SOP (one from a department other than your own):

- You can **read** the full procedure
- You can view **Version History**
- You **cannot acknowledge** it
- You **cannot submit edits** to it
- The viewer shows a "Read only" badge

This is by design — you are a guest reader of procedures from other teams.

---

## Why Cross-Department Search Exists

Cross-department search is valuable because:

- **Knowledge sharing** — Teams often have procedures that are useful to others
- **Process understanding** — See how other departments handle similar tasks
- **Compliance coverage** — QA needs to see all SOPs org-wide

<Callout type="info">
  Only Active SOPs appear in cross-department search. Draft and pending SOPs from other departments are not visible — by design. This prevents seeing work-in-progress from other teams.
</Callout>
