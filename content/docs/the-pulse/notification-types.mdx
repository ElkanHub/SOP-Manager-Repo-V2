Every item in the Pulse has a type that determines how it looks, who receives it, and what action (if any) you need to take.

---

## Notification Types

### Approval Request

- **When you see it:** A Manager has submitted an SOP for QA review
- **Who receives it:** QA Managers
- **What it looks like:** Blue icon showing the SOP number and title
- **What to do:** Click to open the submission and review it

### Approval Response

- **When you see it:** QA has responded to your submission (approved, changes requested, or rejected)
- **Who receives it:** The Manager who submitted the SOP
- **What it looks like:** Blue icon showing what the QA Manager decided
- **What to do:** Check the response — if changes were requested, update and resubmit

### Signature Required

- **When you see it:** A Change Control needs your digital signature
- **Who receives it:** All Managers required to sign (those in the SOP's department)
- **What it looks like:** Navy icon with a pen symbol
- **What to do:** Click to open the Change Control, review the document changes, and sign

### Signature Deadline Warning

- **When you see it:** A Change Control is approaching or past its 14-day deadline
- **Who receives it:** Administrators
- **What it looks like:** Red or amber warning icon
- **What to do:** Check which signatures are still missing and consider waiving if someone is unavailable

### Maintenance Due Today

- **When you see it:** A preventive maintenance task is due today
- **Who receives it:** The person assigned to the task
- **What it looks like:** Amber or blue icon with the equipment name
- **What to do:** Complete the maintenance and log it in the Equipment section

### Maintenance Overdue

- **When you see it:** A maintenance task has passed its due date without being completed
- **Who receives it:** The assigned person and their manager
- **What it looks like:** Red icon — this is urgent
- **What to do:** Complete the task as soon as possible

### SOP Updated

- **When you see it:** An SOP in your department has been updated to a new version
- **Who receives it:** Everyone in that department
- **What it looks like:** Green check icon
- **What to do:** Open the SOP, read the updated version, and acknowledge it

### Notice

- **When you see it:** An announcement has been broadcast
- **Who receives it:** The target audience (your department or everyone)
- **What it looks like:** Grey bell icon
- **What to do:** Read the notice — you can reply once if needed

### New Message

- **When you see it:** Someone has sent you a message
- **Who receives it:** Everyone in the conversation
- **What it looks like:** Chat bubble icon
- **What to do:** Click to open the conversation in Messages

### Personal To-Do

- **When you see it:** You added a personal reminder for yourself
- **Who receives it:** Only you
- **What it looks like:** Purple checklist icon
- **What to do:** Complete the task and check it off

---

<Callout type="tip">
  Items with a red or orange dot are in the Priority group and always appear at the top of the Pulse. Check these first — they often involve actions that other people are waiting on.
</Callout>
