Personal to-dos help you keep track of tasks that are important to you but do not fit into the standard PM system. They are private — only you can see them.

---

## What Are Personal To-Dos?

Personal to-dos are simple reminders you add for yourself:

- Track what you are waiting on from colleagues
- Remind yourself to follow up on something
- Note items to review later

They appear in the **Personal** group in the Pulse.

---

## Adding a To-Do

1. At the bottom of the Pulse, find the to-do input
2. Type your task
3. Click **Add** or press Enter

The to-do appears in your Personal list, marked as pending.

---

## Completing a To-Do

1. Find the to-do in your Personal list
2. Click the checkbox on the left

The to-do is marked as complete and moves to the completed state.

---

## Deleting a To-Do

1. Hover over the to-do item
2. Click the X button on the right

The to-do is removed entirely.

---

## Use Cases

- *"Follow up with John about the equipment log"*
- *"Review the new safety procedure next week"*
- *"Check if my SOP submission was approved"*

These are things the system cannot track automatically — personal reminders that help you stay on top of your work.
