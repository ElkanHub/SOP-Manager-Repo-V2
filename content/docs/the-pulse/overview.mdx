The Pulse is the **primary work interface** of SOP-Guard Pro. Unlike a traditional notification drawer that you open when you remember, the Pulse is always visible on the right side of every page. When something needs your attention, it arrives here.

---

## What is the Pulse?

The Pulse is a real-time feed that shows everything requiring your attention:

- **Approvals** waiting for your review
- **Change Control signatures** you need to provide
- **PM tasks** due today or overdue
- **Notices** broadcast to your department or the whole organisation
- **SOP updates** that have gone active
- **Messages** from colleagues
- **Personal to-dos** you have added

The Pulse never unmounts. It stays visible as you navigate between pages, keeping your work always in context.

---

## The Four Groups

Items in the Pulse are organised into four groups:

### Priority

Items that require **immediate action**:
- Approval requests (you are a QA Manager)
- Change Control signatures due
- Overdue PM tasks
- Deadline escalation warnings

These items have an orange or red indicator and always appear at the top, regardless of when they arrived.

### Today

Items relevant to **today's work**:
- PM tasks due today
- SOPs that have been updated to a new version
- Approval updates (your submission was reviewed)

### Messages

Notices broadcast to your department or everyone, and chat message notifications. Notices are announcements — if you need to respond, use the reply feature inline.

### Personal

Personal to-dos you have added for yourself. Only you can see these.

---

## Real-Time Updates

The Pulse updates in **real-time** via Supabase Realtime. When a new item arrives:

- It appears at the top of the appropriate group
- The unread badge on the bell icon updates
- If you are on a smaller screen where the Pulse is hidden, the badge still shows your unread count

You do not need to refresh the page.

---

## Marking Items as Read

- **Click any item** to mark it as read
- Click **Mark all read** at the top of the Pulse to clear all unread items
- Read items move to their chronological position in the group

---

## The Unread Badge

The teal bell icon in the top navigation shows your unread count at all times. This badge is visible even on smaller screens where the Pulse panel is hidden.

Click the bell on small screens to see a dropdown with your recent items.

---

## Pulse vs Messages

It is important to understand the distinction:

- **The Pulse** is a notification feed. It tells you what happened and what you need to do.
- **Messages** is a conversation system. It is where you chat with colleagues.

When someone sends you a direct message:
1. A Pulse notification appears telling you about it
2. Clicking it takes you to the Messages module to read and respond
3. The conversation itself lives in Messages, not in the Pulse

The Pulse brings work to you; Messages lets you have extended conversations.
