Go to **Settings** → **Departments** to manage your organisation's departments.

---

## Adding a Department

1. Click **Add Department**
2. Enter name (must be unique)
3. Select a colour
4. Click **Create**

---

## Editing a Department

- **Name** — Cannot be changed (by design)
- **Colour** — Can be changed

---

## Deleting a Department

You cannot delete a department if it has:
- Active users
- Active SOPs

The error message shows the counts blocking deletion.

<Callout type="danger">
  Department names cannot be changed after creation. Choose carefully — the name appears in badges throughout the app and in all historical reports. If you need to correct a department name, contact your platform support team.
</Callout>

The **QA** department cannot be deleted.
