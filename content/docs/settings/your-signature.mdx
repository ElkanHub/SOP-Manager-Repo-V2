Your digital signature is used when signing Change Controls.

---

## Viewing Your Signature

Go to **Settings** → **My Profile**. Your signature is shown in the profile summary.

---

## Re-drawing Your Signature

1. Go to **Settings** → **My Profile**
2. Click **Re-draw Signature**
3. Draw or upload a new signature
4. Save

---

## Important

<Callout type="info">
  Your signature is stored securely and only accessible by the platform. It is never shared externally.
</Callout>

Updating your signature affects **future** Change Controls only. Past signature certificates are immutable.
