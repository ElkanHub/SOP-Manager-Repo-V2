Control your notification delivery preferences.

---

## Two Toggles

- **In-app Pulse** — Show notifications in the Pulse panel
- **Email** — Receive email notifications

---

## Recommendation

Keep both on for the best experience. You will see notifications in the app and receive email reminders.

---

## When You Might Disable

- If you receive too many emails, consider disabling email
- You can always check the Pulse manually
