You can choose how you want to receive notifications about activity in the app.

---

## How to Access

1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon in the sidebar)
2. Click the **Notifications** tab

---

## Available Options

There are two notification channels, each with its own toggle:

| Channel | What It Does | Default |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **In-App Pulse** | Shows notifications in the Pulse panel (the right-side feed) | On |
| **Email** | Sends email notifications for important events | On |

---

## Recommendations

- **Keep both on** for the best experience. You will see alerts immediately in the Pulse panel, and get email reminders for anything you might miss.
- **Turn off email** if you check the app frequently and find the email notifications redundant.
- **Never turn off both** — you could miss important approvals, signature requests, or overdue tasks that need your attention.

<Callout type="warning">
  Even with email turned off, you will still receive critical security-related emails (like password reset requests). Those are system-level and cannot be disabled.
</Callout>

---

## What Triggers Notifications

Not everything creates a notification. You will be notified about:

- Approval requests and responses (for QA Managers and Managers)
- Change Control signature requests
- Maintenance tasks due or overdue
- New messages and @mentions
- Notices broadcast to your department or the whole organisation
- SOP updates that require your acknowledgement

Routine actions like viewing a record or editing your profile do not generate notifications.
