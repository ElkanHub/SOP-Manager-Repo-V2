Your profile contains the personal information that appears throughout the app — on submissions, messages, signatures, and anywhere your name is shown.

---

## How to Access Your Profile

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar (the gear icon at the bottom)
2. You will land on the **My Profile** tab by default

---

## What You Can Edit

| Field | Required | Where It Appears |
|-------|----------|------------------|
| Full Name | Yes | Everywhere — submissions, messages, reports, signatures |
| Job Title | Yes | Your profile card and SOP submissions |
| Employee ID | No | Internal reference only |
| Phone | No | Visible to administrators |

### Changing Your Avatar

- Click your current profile photo (or the placeholder circle)
- Select an image file (PNG or JPG, max 2MB)
- Your new photo will appear across the app after saving

---

## Saving Changes

After editing any field, click **Save Changes** at the bottom of the form. You will see a confirmation message when the update is successful.

---

## What You Cannot Change Yourself

Some settings can only be changed by an Administrator:

- **Department** — Contact your admin if you have been moved to a different team
- **Role** (Employee / Manager) — Contact your admin if your role has changed
- **Admin access** — Only another admin can grant or revoke admin privileges

If any of these are incorrect, reach out to your organisation's administrator to have them updated.
