Go to **Settings** → **My Profile** to update your information.

---

## What You Can Edit

- Full Name
- Job Title
- Employee ID (optional)
- Phone (optional)
- Avatar photo

---

## Saving Changes

Click **Save Changes** to update your profile.

---

## What You Cannot Change

- **Department** — Contact admin
- **Role** — Contact admin
- **Admin status** — Contact another admin
