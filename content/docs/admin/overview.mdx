As an Administrator, you have access to features that help you manage the organisation's structure and keep the platform running smoothly.

---

## What Administrators Do

Unlike Managers and QA personnel who work with documents and equipment day-to-day, Administrators handle the setup and configuration of the platform:

- **Manage Users** — View all users, change their roles or departments, grant or revoke admin access, and deactivate accounts when people leave
- **Manage Departments** — Create new departments and set their display colours
- **Review Audit Logs** — See a permanent record of all significant actions taken in the system
- **Monitor System Health** — Understand how your data is stored and protected

---

## Where to Find Admin Features

Admin features are accessed through the **Settings** page (the gear icon in the sidebar). When you open Settings, you will see tabs for:

- **My Profile** — Your personal information (available to everyone)
- **Signature** — Your digital signature (available to everyone)
- **Notifications** — Your notification preferences (available to everyone)
- **Departments** — Create and manage departments (admin only)
- **Users** — Manage all user accounts (admin only)

The Departments and Users tabs are only visible to administrators.

---

## Key Points

<Callout type="info">
  Admin is an extra permission, not a separate role. You are still an Employee or Manager with all the usual capabilities — admin access simply adds the ability to manage users, departments, and view audit logs on top of your existing role.
</Callout>
