SOP-Guard Pro stores all of your organisation's data securely in the cloud. This page explains how your data is protected and what you should be aware of as an administrator.

---

## Automatic Backups

Your data is backed up automatically every day. This includes:

- All SOP documents and their version history
- Equipment records and maintenance logs
- User profiles and signatures
- Audit logs and compliance records

You do not need to run backups manually — this is handled by the platform infrastructure.

---

## Where Your Data is Stored

All data is stored across two systems:

- **Database** — User profiles, SOP metadata, equipment records, maintenance logs, approvals, and audit trails
- **File Storage** — Uploaded SOP documents (.docx files), equipment photos, maintenance completion photos, and digital signatures

Both systems are hosted in secure cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit.

---

## Storage Considerations

<Callout type="warning">
  If your organisation uploads many large SOP documents or high-resolution equipment photos, keep an eye on your storage usage. Contact your platform provider if you approach your storage limit.
</Callout>

To keep storage manageable:
- Use compressed images (PNG or JPG) when uploading equipment photos
- Avoid uploading unnecessarily large Word documents with embedded high-resolution images

---

## Account Access Issues

If a user reports that they cannot access the platform despite having the correct role and department, check the following:

1. **Is their account active?** — Go to **Settings** → **Users** and confirm their status is not set to Deactivated
2. **Have they completed onboarding?** — New users must complete all 4 onboarding steps before they can access the main app
3. **Is their department correct?** — The wrong department means they may not see the SOPs or equipment they expect
