The Audit Log is a permanent, unchangeable record of every important action taken in SOP-Guard Pro. It exists so your organisation can always prove who did what and when — essential for compliance and investigations.

---

## What Gets Recorded

The following actions are logged automatically:

- **SOP activity** — Submissions, approvals, rejections, and version changes
- **Equipment activity** — New equipment additions and approvals
- **Change Control activity** — When Change Controls are issued, signed, or waived
- **User changes** — Account creations, role changes, admin grants, and deactivations
- **Department changes** — When departments are created or modified

---

## What Is NOT Recorded

Everyday actions like the following are not tracked in the Audit Log:

- Messages and personal to-dos
- Calendar events
- Logging in and out
- Simply viewing a record

---

## Who Can Access the Audit Log

- **QA Managers** — Full access to all audit records
- **Administrators** — Full access to all audit records

Other users cannot see the Audit Log.

---

## Using the Audit Log

You can browse audit entries directly in the app, or export them via **Reports** for external analysis. The exported data is available in CSV format, which can be opened in Excel or any spreadsheet tool.

---

## Why Records Cannot Be Changed

Once something is written to the Audit Log, it can never be edited or deleted — not even by administrators. This is by design. A tamper-proof record is a key requirement for regulatory compliance. If an auditor asks "who approved this SOP on March 15th?", the answer is always available and trustworthy.
