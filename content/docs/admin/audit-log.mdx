The audit log is an **append-only** record of all significant actions.

---

## What Is Recorded

- SOP submissions and approvals
- Equipment additions and approvals
- Change Control issuances and signatures
- User account changes
- Admin grants and revocations
- Department changes

---

## What Is NOT Recorded

- Messages and to-dos
- Calendar events
- Session activity (login/logout)
- View actions

---

## Access

- **QA Managers** — Full access
- **Admins** — Full access

---

## Using for Audits

Export via **Reports** for external analysis. The data is in CSV format for easy import.

---

## Append-Only Guarantee

The audit log is enforced at the database level — no UPDATE or DELETE policies exist. Records cannot be modified or removed.
