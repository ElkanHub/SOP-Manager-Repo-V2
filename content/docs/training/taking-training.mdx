# Completing Your Training

SOP Manager makes it easy to stay compliant and up-to-date with standard operating procedures by offering digital training directly in the app.

## The "My Training" Hub

When you are assigned a new training module, you will receive a notification in **The Pulse** and an event will appear on your calendar if there is a deadline. 

To view your training, click on **My Training** in the sidebar navigation (look for the graduation cap icon).

### Viewing Pending Assignments
At the top of the **My Training** page, you'll see a card showing your pending tasks. Each card tells you:
- The title of the training module.
- The related SOP number and version.
- Whether it is Mandatory or Optional.
- The due date (if any). Overdue modules will show a red warning.

## Taking an Assessment

1. **Start the Quiz:** Click the "Start" or "Resume" button on a pending assignment card.
2. **Review the Slides:** First, you will be taken through an automated slide deck that summarizes the key points of the SOP. Use the "Next" and "Previous" buttons to study the material.
3. **Start the Quiz:** After the last slide, click "Start Quiz".
4. **Answer the Questions:** Read each question carefully and select or type your answer. 
5. **Submit:** Once you've answered the questions, click **Submit Assessment** at the bottom of the screen.

### Results & Certificates
Immediately after submitting, you'll see your results. 
- If you **pass**, the assignment moves to your "Completed" list on the My Training page. 
- If you **fail**, you may be required to take the assessment again.

For any passed assessment, you can go to the "Completed" section of the My Training hub and click **Certificate** to download a personalized PDF Certificate of Completion for your records.
