# Managing Training Modules

As a Manager or QA Manager, you have access to the **Training Hub** (accessible from the sidebar). Here, you can convert your active SOPs into comprehensive training materials and assign them to your staff.

## Creating a Training Module

A Training Module is linked directly to an active SOP in your department.

1. Go to the **Training Hub**.
2. Click the **Create Module** button in the top right.
3. **Fill out the Details:** Provide a clear title, select the source SOP from the dropdown list, and enter a short description.
4. **Settings:** Choose if this training is mandatory. You can optionally set a default deadline for when employees must complete the training.
5. Click **Create Module**.

## Generating AI Training Materials

Once your module is created, it will be in **Draft** mode. You must use our AI system to generate the content.

### Generating the Slide Deck
In the module view, go to the **Slide Deck** tab and click **Generate Slides with AI**.
The system reads the associated SOP and creates a presentation summary. 
*Note: You can easily edit the slides and presenter notes by hovering over a slide and clicking "Edit". You can also export them to PowerPoint by clicking "Export PPTX".*

### Generating the Assessment (Quiz)
Next, go to the **Questionnaires** tab to create a test.
1. Enter the number of questions you want.
2. Enter the minimum passing score (e.g. 70%).
3. Click **Generate New Assessment**.
The AI creates a set of multiple-choice, true/false, and short-answer questions. 

**Publish & Lock:** Review the generated quiz. If it looks good, click **Publish & Lock**. This finalizes the quiz so it can be taken by employees. *(If you want, you can also download a paper PDF version).*

## Publishing and Assigning 

After you have generated your slides and successfully published a questionnaire, click the large **Publish Module** button at the top of the page.

Now, your module is active and ready to assign!

1. Go to the **Assignments** tab (or click "Assign Trainees" at the top).
2. Check the boxes next to the employees who need to take this training.
3. Click **Assign Trainees**.

This will immediately send a Pulse notification to those users with a link to their "My Training" hub. As employees complete the training, you can track their progression and completion rates on the module dashboard or securely query the **Training Log** report in the Reports section.
