When a Change Control requires your signature, you receive a Pulse notification. Here is how to sign.

---

## Receiving the Signing Request

When a CC is issued:
- You receive a notification in your Pulse saying a signature is needed
- The notification shows the SOP title and department
- Click to open the Change Control directly

Or find it in the SOP Library — open the SOP and look for the "Change Control in Progress" badge.

---

## The Signing Process

1. Open the Change Control
2. Review the **document comparison** (diff) in the left panel
3. Read the AI summary for context
4. Click **Sign Document**
5. A confirmation modal appears showing your stored signature
6. Click **Confirm & Sign** to complete

Your signature is now recorded in the certificate.

---

## After You Sign

- Your name appears in the signature grid as signed
- The progress bar updates
- If all required signatures are collected, the CC completes automatically

---

## What Happens When Complete

When all signatures are collected:

1. The new SOP version becomes **Active**
2. The old version becomes **Superseded**
3. A Pulse notification goes to the department: "SOP Updated: [title]"
4. All department members must acknowledge the new version

<Callout type="danger">
  Your digital signature is legally binding within your organisation. Do not sign without reading the document changes. The AI summary is a guide only.
</Callout>
