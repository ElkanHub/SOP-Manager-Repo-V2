The Change Control includes a **diff viewer** that shows exactly what changed between the old and new versions of the SOP. This helps you understand what you are signing.

---

## The Diff Viewer

The diff viewer has two columns:

- **Left** — The old (previous) version
- **Right** — The new (updated) version

Changes are highlighted:
- **Red text/highlighting** — Content that was removed or changed
- **Green text/highlighting** — Content that was added or changed

---

## Show All vs Changes Only

Use the toggle to control what you see:

- **Show All** — See the entire document with changes highlighted
- **Changes Only** — Jump straight to the changed sections

---

## AI Summary

An AI-generated summary (using Google Gemini) provides a quick overview of the changes in bullet point format.

<Callout type="warning">
  The AI summary is a convenience tool, not a legal record. Always read the actual diff before signing. Your signature confirms you reviewed the document changes, not just the AI summary.
</Callout>

---

## Reading the Diff

Key things to look for:

1. **New steps added** — Are they clear and correct?
2. **Steps removed** — Why were they removed? Is anything critical missing?
3. **Changed procedures** — Do the new steps make sense?
4. **Safety implications** — Do the changes affect safety procedures?

Your signature confirms you have reviewed and understood these changes.
