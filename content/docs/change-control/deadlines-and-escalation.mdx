Every Change Control has a deadline. Understanding deadlines helps you stay on track.

---

## The 14-Day Deadline

All Change Controls have a **14-day deadline** from creation. This is set automatically when the CC is issued.

---

## Deadline Display Colours

The deadline shown on the CC uses colour coding:

- **Green** — More than 3 days remaining
- **Amber** — 3 days or less remaining
- **Red** — Deadline passed (OVERDUE)

---

## What Happens When Deadline Passes

When the deadline passes:

1. **Admins receive** a Pulse notification (type: cc_deadline)
2. The CC shows as **overdue**
3. Admins should review and either:
   - Wait for outstanding signatures (if close)
   - Waive signatures for unavailable managers

---

## What Non-Admins See

If you are not an Admin:
- You see the deadline and its colour
- You cannot take action on overdue CCs
- You should complete your signature if pending

If you are a required signatory and the CC is overdue, complete your signature as soon as possible.
