PM dates appear automatically on the calendar based on equipment schedules.

---

## How PM Dates Appear

When equipment is approved, PM tasks are created for each cycle. These tasks appear on the calendar at their due dates.

---

## Clicking a PM Chip

Click any PM chip to see:
- Which equipment
- Who is assigned
- Due date

---

## Using the Calendar for Planning

The calendar helps you plan maintenance:
- See upcoming PM workload
- Plan resources around due dates
- Identify busy periods
