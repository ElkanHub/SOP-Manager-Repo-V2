The **Calendar** shows two types of entries:

1. **PM Dates** — Automatic entries from the equipment schedule
2. **Manual Events** — Events created by users

---

## Chip Colours

- **Blue** — PM dates
- **Green** — Your department's manual events
- **Grey** — Public events (from other departments)

---

## Navigation

- Navigate months with arrows
- Click a date to see details
- Today is highlighted

---

## Upcoming Panel

The right side shows upcoming events for the next 7 days.
