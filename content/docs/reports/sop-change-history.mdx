The SOP Change History report shows every version change for every SOP.

---

## What It Shows

- SOP Number and Title
- Version change (e.g., v1.0 → v1.1)
- Changed by (who submitted the update)
- Date of change
- Change type (New or Update)

---

## Use Cases

- **Regulatory audits** — Prove when procedures were updated
- **Change tracking** — See the evolution of any SOP
- **Compliance verification** — Confirm all SOPs were properly approved
