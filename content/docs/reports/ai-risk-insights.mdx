AI Risk Insights uses Google Gemini to analyze your SOPs and identify potential risks.

---

## What It Is

The AI reviews your Active SOPs and provides:
- A risk level assessment (Low/Medium/High)
- Bullet points describing potential risks
- Suggestions for improvement

---

## How to Generate

1. Go to Reports → AI Risk Insights
2. Click **Generate Insights**
3. Wait for analysis to complete

Results are cached — if you run again within 24 hours, you see cached results. Click **Regenerate** for fresh analysis.

---

## Risk Levels

- **Green** — Low risk
- **Amber** — Medium risk
- **Red** — High risk
