The Acknowledgement Log shows every acknowledgement ever made.

---

## What It Shows

- SOP Number and Title
- Version acknowledged
- Who acknowledged
- When acknowledged

---

## Use Cases

- **Compliance audits** — Prove who has read critical procedures
- **Follow-up** — See who still needs to acknowledge an update
- **Verification** — Confirm your team has read new procedures

<Callout type="tip">
  After a Change Control completes, run this report filtered to that SOP to track who has and has not acknowledged the new version.
</Callout>
