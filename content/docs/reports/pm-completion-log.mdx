The PM Completion Log shows every PM task that was completed.

---

## What It Shows

- Asset ID and Name
- Assigned To (who was responsible)
- Completed By (who actually did it)
- Completion date
- Notes from the completion
- Photo (if uploaded)

---

## Note

**Assigned To** and **Completed By** are separate fields. The task may be assigned to one person but completed by another — both are recorded.

---

## Use Cases

- **Compliance** — Prove maintenance was performed
- **Audit** — Show all maintenance history
- **Trends** — Identify equipment with issues
