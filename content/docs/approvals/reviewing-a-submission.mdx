When you click to review a submission, you see a two-panel layout:

- **Left panel** — The submitted document
- **Right panel** — Details and actions

---

## Left Panel: The Document

This shows the **submitted file** exactly as the Manager uploaded it. It is not the current live version — it is the new version being proposed.

Review it carefully:
- Read through the entire procedure
- Check for clarity and completeness
- Verify it follows your organisation's standards

---

## Right Panel: Submission Details

The right panel shows:

- **Submitter info** — Name, department, role, when submitted
- **Submission history** — All previous submissions for this SOP
- **Notes to QA** — Any notes the submitter included
- **Review thread** — Comments from previous reviews

---

## The Review Thread

The review thread shows the history:

- Every time you request changes or approve, it is recorded
- Every resubmission is tracked
- Comments are preserved

This lets you see the full context of the submission.

---

## Starting Your Review

1. Open the submission
2. Read the document in the left panel
3. Check the Notes to QA for context
4. Decide: Approve, Request Changes, or Reject
