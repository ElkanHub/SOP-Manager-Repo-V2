The **Approvals** module is where QA Managers review and approve SOP submissions. This is the gateway that ensures every Active SOP in the organisation meets quality standards.

---

## Who Can Access Approvals

The Approvals page is **only visible to QA Managers**. It is hidden from Employees and non-QA Managers.

When you have pending approvals, the page appears in your sidebar navigation with a badge showing the count.

---

## The Approval Queue

The main Approvals page shows:

- **Pending** — Submissions waiting for your review
- **Changes Requested** — Your feedback has been sent, awaiting resubmission
- **Approved** — Submissions you have approved
- **Rejected** — Submissions that were rejected

Each submission appears as a card showing:
- The SOP number and title
- Who submitted it
- When it was submitted
- Type (New or Update)
- Current status

---

## Submission History

All submissions are tracked:

- Every resubmission after "changes requested" creates a new entry
- The full history is preserved
- You can see the entire thread of comments and responses

This creates a clear audit trail of the review process.

---

## Self-Approval Block

<Callout type="danger">
  You cannot approve a SOP you submitted yourself. The system rejects self-approval automatically.
</Callout>

If you submitted an SOP yourself, you will see it in the queue but you cannot approve it. Another QA Manager must handle it.
