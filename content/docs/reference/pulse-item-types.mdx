Use this table as a quick reference for every type of notification you might see in the Pulse panel.

## Item Types

| Notification | What It Means | Pulse Group |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Approval Request | An SOP has been submitted for QA review | Priority |
| Approval Response | QA has responded to your submission | Today |
| Signature Required | A Change Control needs your signature | Priority |
| Signature Deadline | Change Control deadline is approaching or passed | Priority |
| Maintenance Due | A maintenance task is due today | Today |
| Maintenance Overdue | A maintenance task is past its due date | Priority |
| SOP Updated | A new version of an SOP is now active | Today |
| Notice | An announcement was broadcast | Messages |
| New Message | You received a chat message | Messages |
| Personal To-Do | A reminder you created for yourself | Personal |

<Callout type="tip">
  Items in the **Priority** group always appear at the top of the Pulse, no matter when they arrived. These are the most important — they usually require your action and may be blocking other people's work.
</Callout>
