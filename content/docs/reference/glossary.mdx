## Terms

**Acknowledgement** — A formal record confirming that you have read and understood a specific version of an SOP. You create this by clicking the **Acknowledge** button when reading a procedure.

**Active** — The current, live status of an SOP or piece of equipment. Active SOPs are visible to all users in the relevant department and can be found via search.

**Admin** — An extra permission that gives a user access to administrative features like managing users, departments, and viewing audit logs. Admin can be added to either Employee or Manager accounts.

**Change Control (CC)** — The formal sign-off process that happens when an existing SOP is updated. All affected Managers must digitally sign the Change Control before the new version becomes Active.

**Dashboard** — The home page you see when you log in. Shows key numbers and recent activity relevant to your role.

**Department** — Your team in the organisation. It determines which SOPs and equipment you see by default when you open the Library or Equipment pages.

**Draft** — An SOP that has been created but not yet submitted for QA review. Only visible to Managers in the owning department.

**Employee** — A role for users who read and acknowledge SOPs, complete maintenance tasks, and receive notices. The default role for most users.

**Manager** — A role for users who can do everything an Employee can, plus submit SOPs, sign Change Controls, add equipment, and reassign maintenance tasks.

**Pending QA** — The status of an SOP or piece of equipment that has been submitted for review and is waiting for a QA Manager to approve it.

**PM (Preventive Maintenance)** — Scheduled maintenance tasks for equipment. PM tasks appear in the Equipment section and on the Calendar.

**Pulse** — The notification panel on the right side of the app that shows everything needing your attention — approvals, tasks, messages, and personal to-dos. Updates automatically in real-time.

**QA Manager** — A user in the QA department with the Manager role. QA Managers are the only users who can approve SOP submissions and equipment additions.

**Role** — Either Employee or Manager. Your role determines what actions you can take in the app.

**Secondary Department** — An additional department that a user or piece of equipment belongs to, beyond their primary department. Users with secondary department access can view and work with records from those departments too.

**Superseded** — The status of an old SOP version that has been replaced by a newer Active version. You can still view superseded versions through the Version History feature.
