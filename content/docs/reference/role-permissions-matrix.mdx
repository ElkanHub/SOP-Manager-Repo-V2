## Understanding the Roles

- **Employee** — The standard role for most users. Focused on reading procedures, acknowledging them, and completing assigned maintenance tasks.

- **Manager** — Includes everything an Employee can do, plus the ability to submit SOPs, sign Change Controls, add equipment, and reassign tasks.

- **QA Manager** — A Manager in the QA department. Has the unique ability to approve SOP submissions and equipment additions across all departments.

- **Admin** — Not a separate role, but an extra permission. Adds the ability to manage users, departments, and view audit logs. Can be added to either Employee or Manager accounts.

<Callout type="info">
  If you believe your permissions are incorrect — for example, you are a Manager but cannot submit SOPs — contact your administrator to verify your role and department settings.
</Callout>
