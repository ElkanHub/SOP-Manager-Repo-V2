Welcome to SOP-Guard Pro. This guide walks you through the essential tasks to start using the platform effectively.

<Callout type="tip">
  You do not need to learn everything immediately. The platform reveals more as your work requires it. Start with reading SOPs and acknowledging them — everything else follows naturally from there.
</Callout>

<StepList>
<Step number={1} title="Sign in and complete onboarding (2 minutes)">

Your administrator creates your account and you receive an email invitation. On your first sign-in, the **4-step onboarding wizard** launches automatically.

Complete all four steps:
1. **Department and Role** — Select your department and whether you are a Manager or Employee
2. **Profile Details** — Enter your full name, job title, and optionally your employee ID and phone
3. **Digital Signature** — Draw your signature on the canvas or upload an image (required for signing Change Controls)
4. **Review and Confirm** — Review your details and complete setup

After clicking **Complete Setup**, you land on the Dashboard.

</Step>
<Step number={2} title="Understand the Dashboard (1 minute)">

The Dashboard shows you at a glance:

- **KPI cards** — Four numbers relevant to your role (pending approvals, active SOPs, upcoming PM tasks, etc.)
- **Activity feed** — Recent events in your department
- **The Pulse** — The right-side panel showing what needs your attention

Spend a moment familiarising yourself with the layout. The Pulse will be visible on every page.

</Step>
<Step number={3} title="Explore the SOP Library (3 minutes)">

Navigate to **SOP Library** in the sidebar.

- The table shows all **Active SOPs** in your department
- Click any row to open it in the tab viewer
- Read through an SOP. If there is an **Acknowledge** button in the header bar, click it to confirm you have read this version
- Try the **search bar** at the top — type any keyword and results appear from across the whole organisation

The SOP Library is your central hub for finding and reading procedures.

</Step>
<Step number={4} title="Check the Pulse (1 minute)">

Look at the right-side panel. Items are grouped:

- **Priority** — Action required now (approvals, signatures, overdue tasks)
- **Today** — Informational items relevant today
- **Messages** — Notices and chat notifications
- **Personal** — Your own to-dos

> **Priority items always appear at the top** regardless of when they arrived. Check this group first — these items can block other people's work if you delay.

The teal bell icon in the top navigation shows your unread count at all times, even when the Pulse panel is hidden on smaller screens.

</Step>
<Step number={5} title="Send your first message (2 minutes)">

Navigate to **Messages** in the sidebar.

- Click **New** → **Direct Message**
- Search for a colleague by name
- Send a message

Try clicking the **paperclip icon** to reference an SOP from the library inline. Your colleague can then click **Open** to read the SOP directly from the conversation.

</Step>
</StepList>

---

You are now set up and ready to use SOP-Guard Pro. As your role requires, you will discover more features:

- If you are a **Manager**, you can submit SOPs and sign Change Controls
- If you are in **QA**, you will see the Approvals queue
- If you are an **Administrator**, you can manage users and departments in Settings
