When you first sign in to SOP-Guard Pro, you are guided through a 4-step onboarding wizard. This ensures your profile is complete and you understand your role in the system.

<Callout type="warning">
  If your department or role is wrong, do not continue using the platform with incorrect settings — contact your administrator immediately. Department and role determine what you see and what you can do throughout the entire platform.
</Callout>

---

## Step 1 — Department and Role

### What is a Department?

Your **department** is the team you belong to. It determines which SOPs and equipment you see by default when you open the Library or Equipment pages. Your department appears as a badge on your profile throughout the platform.

### What is Your Role?

- **Employee** — You can read SOPs, acknowledge them, complete PM tasks, and send notices. This is the default role for most users.

- **Manager** — Everything an Employee can do, plus:
  - Submit SOPs for approval
  - Sign Change Controls when SOPs are updated
  - Add equipment to the registry
  - Reassign PM tasks

<Callout type="info">
  The warning that appears when you select Manager is intentional. Managers have additional responsibilities, including the legal obligation to sign Change Controls when SOPs are updated.
</Callout>

If your department or role is incorrect, contact your administrator to fix it before proceeding.

---

## Step 2 — Profile Details

Fill in your profile information:

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Full Name | Yes | Displayed throughout the platform |
| Job Title | Yes | Shown on your profile and in submissions |
| Employee ID | No | Your internal staff number |
| Phone | No | For internal directories |

### Uploading Your Avatar

- Click the upload button to select a photo
- Use a PNG or JPG file, maximum 2MB
- The preview shows a circle — crop or adjust as needed

---

## Step 3 — Digital Signature

Your **digital signature** is used when you sign a Change Control. It is a legally binding confirmation that you have reviewed and approved document changes.

### Drawing Your Signature

- Use your mouse or touchscreen to draw in the canvas
- Draw clearly — your signature will be stored as an image
- Click **Clear** to start over if needed

### Uploading a Signature

- Click the **Upload** tab
- Drag and drop an image or click to browse
- Use a PNG or JPG file, maximum 2MB
- Transparent backgrounds work best

<Callout type="info">
  The Continue button is disabled until a signature is captured. This is required — you cannot skip this step.
</Callout>

---

## Step 4 — Review and Confirm

Review your completed profile:

- Your name and job title
- Your department badge
- Your role badge (Employee or Manager)
- Your signature confirmation

Click **Complete Setup** to finish onboarding. You land on the Dashboard.

---

## Updating Your Signature Later

Your signature can be updated at any time:

1. Go to **Settings** → **My Profile**
2. Click **Re-draw Signature**
3. The same canvas interface opens
4. Draw or upload your new signature

> **Note:** Updating your signature affects future Change Controls only. Past signature certificates remain unchanged — they are immutable records of what you signed at the time.
