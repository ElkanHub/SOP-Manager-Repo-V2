Every user in SOP-Guard Pro has three attributes that determine what they can see and do:

1. **Department** — The team you belong to
2. **Role** — Either Employee or Manager
3. **Admin flag** — Whether you have administrative access

These attributes combine to define your effective permissions.

---

## The Three Attributes

### Department

Your department determines which SOPs and equipment you see by default. When you view the SOP Library, you initially see only SOPs from your department. Cross-department search lets you find SOPs from other teams, but you can only acknowledge SOPs from your own department.

### Role

- **Employee** — Read SOPs, acknowledge them, complete PM tasks, send notices and messages. The platform shows you what is relevant to your work.

- **Manager** — Everything an Employee can do, plus:
  - Submit SOPs for QA approval
  - Sign Change Controls when SOPs are updated
  - Add equipment to the registry
  - Reassign PM tasks to other team members

### Admin Flag

The Admin flag is an **additional layer** of capability that can be added to either Employee or Manager accounts. It adds:

- User management (invite, edit, deactivate users)
- Department management (add, edit departments)
- Full audit log access

<Callout type="info">
  Admin does **not** override your operational role. An Admin+Employee still cannot sign a Change Control — that requires the Manager role. Admin simply adds administrative capabilities on top of what your role already allows.
</Callout>

---

## QA Managers

**QA is a department designation, not a separate role.**

A **QA Manager** is a user who:
1. Is in the QA department
2. Has the Manager role
3. Therefore can approve SOPs and equipment

QA Managers have unique visibility:
- They can see **all SOPs** across all departments
- They can see **all equipment** across all departments
- They are the **sole approvers** of SOP submissions and equipment additions

---

## Role Combinations

| You Are... | You Can... |
|------------|------------|
| Employee | Read own-dept SOPs, acknowledge, complete PM tasks, send messages |
| Manager | Everything Employee + submit SOPs, sign CCs, add equipment, reassign PM |
| Admin+Employee | Employee + manage users, departments, view audit logs |
| Admin+Manager | Manager + admin capabilities |
| QA Manager (QA dept + Manager) | See all SOPs/equipment, approve submissions |
| QA Admin+Manager | Everything — superuser access |

<Callout type="info">
  You cannot change your own role. If your role is incorrect, contact your administrator.
</Callout>
