Reference records to share context directly in conversations.

---

## How to Reference

1. Click the **paperclip** icon
2. Choose what to reference:
   - SOP
   - Equipment
   - Change Control
3. Search or browse to find the record
4. One reference per message

---

## Reference Cards

The reference appears as a rich card showing:
- Type (SOP/Equipment/CC)
- Title
- Status badge
- **Open** button

---

## Status Updates

If the referenced record changes (e.g., SOP superseded, equipment deactivated), the card updates to show the new status.

<Callout type="tip">
  This is the most powerful feature of messaging in SOP-Guard Pro. Instead of saying "check SOP-007", embed the actual SOP — your colleague clicks Open and reads it immediately, in context, without leaving the conversation.
</Callout>
