Group conversations include 3 or more people.

---

## Creating a Group

1. Go to **Messages**
2. Click **New** → **Group**
3. Add at least 2 other people
4. Give the group a name
5. Click **Create**

---

## Managing a Group

- **Add members** — Click the group header → add people
- **Change name** — Click the group header → edit name
- **Leave** — Click the group header → leave group

---

## Notification Settings

<Callout type="info">
  Unlike direct messages, group conversations can be muted. If a group is very active and you only need to check it periodically, change the notification setting to Muted.
</Callout>
