Direct messages are private conversations between two people.

---

## Starting a DM

1. Go to **Messages**
2. Click **New** → **Direct Message**
3. Search for the person
4. Start typing

---

## Sending Messages

- Type and press **Enter** to send
- Hold **Shift+Enter** for a new line

---

## Editing Messages

- Edit within **15 minutes** of sending
- Click the three dots → **Edit**
- Shows "edited" label after

---

## Deleting Messages

- Click the three dots → **Delete**
- Shows "This message was deleted" replacement
- Recipients can still see it was there
