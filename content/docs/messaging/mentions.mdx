Mentions notify someone even in muted conversations.

---

## How to Mention

1. Type **@** in a message
2. See a list of conversation members
3. Select the person

---

## How Mentions Appear

- Highlighted in **teal**
- Your own mentions are brighter

---

## Notifications

@mentions override mute settings — you will be notified even if the conversation is muted.
