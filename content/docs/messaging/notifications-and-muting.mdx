Control your notification preferences for each conversation.

---

## Notification Options

- **All** — Notify for every message
- **Mentions only** — Only when @mentioned
- **Muted** — No notifications

---

## Changing Settings

1. Open the conversation
2. Click the **settings** icon in the header
3. Choose your preference

---

## DM Notifications

Direct messages **cannot be muted** — you always receive notifications.

---

## Muted Indicator

Muted conversations show a muted icon in the list. The unread count still updates — you just do not get notifications.
